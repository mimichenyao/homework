How It Works

_____________

01

Take our style quiz to match with a personal shopper. You'll connect 1:1 for a consultation over text messaging.

02

She shops the entire Internet, then sends you a virtual Lookbook with clothes that fit your style, body & budget.

03

You purchase what you want right from your Lookbook. Items ship from brands & retailers directly. No box, no surprises.
